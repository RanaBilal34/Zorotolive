Synopsis and Setting

"Zoroto" is set in a dystopian future where humanity faces existential threats from both within and beyond. The story revolves around a group of young individuals who possess extraordinary abilities, known as "Zoros." These abilities, ranging from telekinesis to elemental manipulation, have emerged due to a mysterious phenomenon known as the "Zoroto Burst."
